NIMBUS CLOUDS

1. Nimbus clouds are found at 500 to 5,000 meters above the ground and are spread across the sky uniformly.

2. Nimbus clouds make steady rain or snow, but not thunderstorms or lightning.

3. There are several types of Nimbus clouds, Cumulonimbus and Stratonimbus are the most common.

4. Cumulonimbus clouds are capable of producing lightning, thunder, and tornadoes.

5. The word "Nimbus" means dark cloud in Latin. This is beacuse nimbus clouds are almost always greyish black.
